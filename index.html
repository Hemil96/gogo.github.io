Frequently Asked Questions

How does GoGo's Own As You Drive work?

GoGo's provides you an option to select an uber ready car, make weekly payments and once agreed tenure is over and you are a proud vehicle owner.

Is there a down payment required?

No, there is no down payment required to start your journey to car ownership with GoGo.

What types of vehicles does GoGo offer for Own As You Drive?

GoGo is extremely flexible in providing various types of vehicles, get in touch with GoGo if you have a specific vehicle in mind, and it is not listed on our website as we may be able to source that vehicle for you.

Are there any hidden fees or extra costs?

GoGo is committed to transparency. There are no hidden fees; our pricing is straightforward, and you'll always know what you're paying for.

Does GoGo provide maintenance for the cars?

Yes, GoGo takes care of car maintenance. Our Own As You Drive option includes vehicle registration, maintenance and servicing thought the Own As You Drive tenure.

Is good credit history required for GoGo cars?

No, every application is considered, and good credit history is not a mandatory requirement.

Can my partner drive the car?

Second driver can be nominated and once approved, occasional use is permitted, however only the main driver can use car for Uber.

Is car insured?

Our insurance covers you theft, damage during general or ride sharing usages Full terms are specified in your agreement and are only valid if vehicle is used as per stated terms.

What documents are required?

To sign up, you’ll need a copy of your driver’s licence with your current address or change of address sticker, your passport/birth certificate or citizenship certificate.

Can I drive GoGo car for personal use?

You are welcome to drive car for occasional personal use, however mainly for business. If you want to drive the car on a road trip, please let us know in advance.

Is my wife/husband drive the car?

You can nominate a second driver, and hey can use the car for occasional personal use. However, only one driver may use the car for Uber.

What are the requirements for the Own As You Drive option?

Over 25 years old

Valid Australian drivers' license

Approved rideshare driver with ridesharing company such as Uber, Didi and Ola

What is covered in servicing and general maintenance included?

Your weekly payment covers the following:

- All scheduled and warranty maintenance costs are included in subscription as per manufactured guidelines

- Roadside assistance

- Tyres

It is customers responsibility to cover all repairs not covered by warranty, it includes but not limited to tyre punctures, panel damage, and all other non-wear-and-tear items.

Set up cost for own as drive option?

Based on select car, a weekly payment needs to be paid in advance for upcoming week. There is a setup fee to join oue Own As You Drive option. This fee covers your car’s delivery, contract documentation and direct debit set up activities.

What is covered by weekly payment?

The weekly payment covers car registration, general maintenance as per manufacturer guideline, insurance, and roadside assistance.

Is there any limit on how much can I drive?

Weekly payment includes 1,000 kms driving allowance per week and any excess kilometers will incur 10 cent per km

Any penalty for missed payment ?

GoGo will charge $25 overdue payment fee for any missed payments. If you think there could be issues with upcoming payment, please let us know in advance so alternative payment options can be considered.
