Making vehicle ownership possible

A friend going through tough times and unable to get a vehicle finance led to an idea about GoGo, our journey started with helping a friend in need whilst keeping their dignity intact. We believe vehicle ownership is a necessity not a privilege, and our mission is to make vehicle ownership a reality for everyone regardless of financial situation or credit history.

We understand traditional car buying process can be daunting usually with hefty down payments especially for those with limited financial resources or past credit challenges. This challenge inspired us to create a unique customer centric solution we call ‘Own as you drive'.

What Makes Us Unique

Inclusiveness

We strongly believe every Australian deserve an opportunity to own a reliable vehicle. Our own as you drive program is designed to be inclusive, provide car ownership for an underserved and often neglected section of the market by traditional financial institutions.

Flexibility

We all know life is unpredictable and that s why we offer flexible payment plans according to your budget and financial situation whilst providing access to a reliable vehicle.

Customer centric approach

Customer satisfaction is extremely important to us, we address your questions and concerns promptly and customer decides the tenure of their vehicle ownership journey.

Transparency

Transparency is core of our business, we provide clear terms, honest pricing, and guidance through the entire process, so our customers exactly know what to expect.

Path to vehicle ownership

Our 'Own As You Drive' model is not about renting a car but rather a pathway to owning a vehicle of your choice. We provide you an opportunity to build equity in your car as you make payments, leading you towards vehicle ownership.
